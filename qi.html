Current Research: Quantum Field Theory via Quantum Computation

My current research is being done with Dr. Gary Goldstein in which we utilize a quantum computation optimization algorithm known as variational quantum eigensolver to found bound states of the Yukawa Hamiltonian of Quantum Field Theory. From here, we were the first group to generate generalized parton distribution functions (GPDs) via quantum computation. The GPD gives the momentum distribution of partons in a hadronic bound state after an interaction, i.e., a pion interacting with a photon.
